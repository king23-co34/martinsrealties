Who we are

D'S Martins Nig Enterprise is a Nigerian company operating across three complementary business lines — property and real estate, oil and gas, and equipment leasing. What began as a real estate practice has grown into a diversified enterprise, while keeping the same client-first, detail-driven approach in every sector we serve.

Whether you're searching for a home in Lagos, sourcing industrial equipment, or looking for a dependable partner in the oil & gas value chain, our team brings hands-on expertise and a modern digital platform to every engagement.

Our approach

Every property listed on our platform is reviewed by our team before publication. Every inquiry — across all three business lines — is tracked from first contact through resolution, and every order is confirmed by an administrator before it's finalised.

We combine hands-on consulting with a modern digital platform, so clients can search, review, and reserve properties with confidence, and reach out just as easily for oil & gas or equipment leasing needs.
